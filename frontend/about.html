About PriceCompare

We help you find the best deals across major e-commerce platforms in India.

Our Mission

At PriceCompare, we believe shopping should be simple and transparent. Our mission is to help you make informed purchasing decisions by providing real-time price comparisons across India's leading online stores.

We scan Amazon, JioMart, Myntra, and Ajio to find you the best deals on everything from groceries to electronics, fashion to home goods.

How It Works

1

Search

Enter the product you're looking for in the search bar. Be as specific as possible for the most accurate results.

2

Compare

We instantly scan across Amazon, JioMart, Myntra, and Ajio to find matching products and their prices.

3

Shop

Compare prices, reviews, and ratings, then click "View Deal" to go directly to the product on the retailer's website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PriceCompare free to use?

Yes, PriceCompare is completely free for all users. We don't charge any fees for using our service.

How accurate are the prices?

We strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date prices possible. However, prices on e-commerce platforms can change rapidly, so we recommend checking the final price on the retailer's website before purchasing.

Do you store my search history?

We do not store your individual search history or personal information. Your privacy is important to us.

Can I filter results by specific criteria?

Yes, you can filter search results by platform and sort by price (low to high, high to low) or by rating.
